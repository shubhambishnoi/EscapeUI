Round Avatars

These avatars are of 3 different sizes i.e large avatar (avatar-lg), medium avatar (avatar-md),

and small avatar (avatar-sm).

Avatars with status

These avatars are also of 3 different sizes i.e large avatar (avatar-lg), medium avatar (avatar-md),

and small avatar (avatar-sm) and they also have status icon present i.e online (status-online), offline (status-offline) and dnd (status-dnd).
